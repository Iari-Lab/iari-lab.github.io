Acoustic Array Technology

Our platform uses distributed microphone arrays to capture spatial sound information across industrial environments, enabling precise localization and identification of acoustic anomalies.

Using advanced beamforming algorithms and machine learning, we can detect equipment failures, leaks, and mechanical issues before they become critical problems.

  • Real-time 3D sound source localization
  • Leak detection
  • Mechanical wear prediction
  • Predictive maintenance insights
